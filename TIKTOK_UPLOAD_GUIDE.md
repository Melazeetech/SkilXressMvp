# TikTok Video Upload Guide

## Overview

Providers can now share their TikTok videos directly on SkilXpress using TikTok links - no need to download and re-upload!

## How to Upload TikTok Videos

### Step 1: Get Your TikTok Video Link

1. Open the TikTok app or website
2. Find the video you want to share
3. Click the "Share" button
4. Click "Copy Link"

The link will look like:
- `https://www.tiktok.com/@yourusername/video/1234567890`
- `https://vm.tiktok.com/ZMxxx/` (short link)

### Step 2: Upload to SkilXpress

1. Go to your **Provider Dashboard**
2. Click **"My Videos"** tab
3. Click **"Add Video"** button
4. Select **"Use URL"** upload method
5. Paste your TikTok link in the "Video URL or TikTok Link" field
6. Fill in title, description, and category
7. Click **"Upload Video"**

## Tips

### For TikTok Videos:
- Make sure the video is **public** (not private)
- Use **vertical videos** for best display in the feed
- Keep videos **under 3 minutes** for best engagement

### For Other Videos:
- Use **direct video file URLs** ending in `.mp4`, `.webm`, etc.
- Make sure the URL is **publicly accessible**
- Test the URL in a browser first to confirm it loads

## Troubleshooting

### "Video doesn't load"
- Check if the TikTok video is public
- Try copying the link again
- Make sure you copied the full URL

### "Auto-fill doesn't work"
- That's OK! Just type your title manually
- Auto-fill is a bonus feature, not required

### "Video shows blank in feed"
- The TikTok link might be invalid
- Try using a different TikTok video
- Contact support if issue persists
